Privacy Policy — Streaks

Last updated: July 4, 2026

Streaks (“the app”, package com.rajat.streaks.app) is a habit tracker that helps you build routines with on-device reminders. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your information. Streaks is designed to be local-first: your habit data stays on your device.

Information the app handles

  • Habit data (stored only on your device). The habits you create — names, emoji, schedules, reminder times, and completion history — are saved locally on your device using the operating system’s storage. This data is not transmitted to us or to any server.
  • Notification token (only if you enable notifications). If you turn on push notifications, the app registers with Expo’s push service and receives an anonymous device push token. This token is used solely to deliver reminders to your device. It contains no name, email, or contact information.
  • Optional crash diagnostics. If crash reporting is enabled in a given build, anonymized crash and performance diagnostics may be sent to Sentry to help fix bugs. Personally identifiable information is not attached (sendDefaultPii: false). Crash reporting is disabled by default.

Information the app does NOT collect

  • No account, sign-in, name, or email is required or collected.
  • No location data.
  • No contacts, photos, or files.
  • No advertising identifiers, and the app shows no ads.
  • No analytics or behavioral tracking across apps or websites.
  • We do not sell or rent any data to anyone.

Notifications

Habit reminders are scheduled as local notifications on your device — they work offline and require no server. Optional push notifications are routed through Expo’s push service (Android FCM / iOS APNs) only to reach your device.

Third-party services

  • Expo — push notification delivery. See expo.dev/privacy.
  • Sentry (optional, off by default) — crash diagnostics. See sentry.io/privacy.

Data retention and deletion

Because habit data lives on your device, you are always in control:

  • Delete a habit inside the app to remove it and cancel its reminders.
  • Uninstalling the app permanently removes all locally stored habit data from your device.
  • The optional backup feature exports your data as JSON to your own clipboard only when you tap it; nothing is uploaded automatically.

Security

Data in transit (the push token and any optional diagnostics) is sent over encrypted HTTPS connections. Habit data at rest is kept in your device’s app-private storage.

Children’s privacy

Streaks is a general-audience utility and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above.
